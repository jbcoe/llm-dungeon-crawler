# NPC Types

- Stranded Engineer: A station technician in a grease-stained jumpsuit who knows the layout of every conduit and vent but refuses to move without tools.
- Rebel Soldier: A member of the outer-colony resistance, armed and suspicious, willing to trade intel for safe passage.
- Rogue AI Terminal: A decommissioned station AI still active on a local node, offering information in exchange for resource uploads.
- Refugee Colonist: A traumatized survivor of an alien attack, barely holding together, clinging to any news of rescue ships.
- Black-Market Trader: A shadowy figure operating out of a concealed cargo module, dealing in contraband tech and forged transit papers.
- Xenoanthropologist: A scientist obsessed with alien culture, currently attempting to communicate with the hive creatures, and too excited to be frightened.
- Corporate Spy: An agent embedded in the station, pretending to be a maintenance worker. Helpful up to the point where interests diverge.
- Uploaded Crew Member: A deceased engineer whose consciousness was uploaded to the station network, existing as a voice in the terminals.
- Alien Ambassador: A non-hostile extraterrestrial communicating through a translation device, seeking to understand why humans are in its space.
- Disgraced Admiral: A former fleet commander hiding here after a catastrophic decision, bitter but possessing invaluable tactical knowledge.
- Medical Drone (Neutral): An autonomous surgical unit running low on power, still programmed to assist the injured — unless disturbed.
- Temporal Echo: A recurring holographic loop of a crew member's final moments before the disaster, sometimes responding to the right prompts.
- Veteran Scavenger: A grizzled loner who has survived here for months, knowing exactly which areas are safe and which are lethal.
- Hive Drone (Passive): A single creature separated from its hive-mind, confused and docile, but liable to turn hostile if the hive is contacted.
- Cryogenically Preserved Passenger: A civilian just thawed from cryo-sleep, completely unaware of the catastrophe, in a state of absolute shock.
- Smuggler Captain: A pragmatic trader who flew into this sector by accident, now desperately trying to repair their ship and leave.
- Quarantine AI: The station's medical intelligence, locked behind a firewall, warning of a biological containment failure in measured, calm tones.
