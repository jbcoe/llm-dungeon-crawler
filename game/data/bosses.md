# Final Bosses

- The Lich King Mordavian: An ancient undead sorcerer who traded his humanity for immortality, commanding legions of the dead from his obsidian throne with cold, absolute authority.
- The Serpent Queen Vaspara: A colossal half-woman, half-snake entity who rules the deepest tunnels, enslaving creatures with her hypnotic gaze and venomous embrace.
- Malachar the Unmade: A demon lord whose form shifts endlessly between flesh, shadow, and void, banished from the abyss and furiously hungry for a new realm to corrupt.
- The Iron Colossus: A titanic war golem crafted by a mad artificer and powered by a trapped elemental soul, now run amok and driven by an insatiable need to destroy all living things.
- The Dread Dragon Xyrathos: An ancient wyrm whose scales have turned to living shadow over millennia of slumber, awakened now and ravenous, whose breath weapon is pure darkness.
- Gorrath the Undying: A hulking orc warlord blessed by a blood god with dark regenerative powers, stitched together by dark magic so that wounds close before they can finish him.
- The Horned God Zharr: A lesser deity of chaos and annihilation who descended into the dungeon seeking mortal worshippers, demanding obeisance through fear and consuming fire.
- The Pale Sorceress Elara: A brilliant necromancer sealed away for a century in punishment for forbidden experiments, now freed and wrathful, wielding the power of death with terrifying precision.
- The Warden of the Deep: An ancient kraken-like entity that has slumbered in the dungeon's innermost sanctum for a thousand years, its tentacles reaching through walls and its mind capable of shattering sanity.
- The Shadow Empress: A dark fey queen banished underground by her own court, her beauty concealing a murderous cunning and the ability to twist shadows into blades with a gesture.
- The Forsaken Paladin: A former champion of light who descended into the dungeon to face its evil alone, was corrupted utterly, and now serves the darkness he once sought to destroy.
- Skavarak the Plague Lord: A skeletal rat-like sorcerer wearing robes of stitched vermin hide, whose every breath carries a contagion potent enough to fell an army.
